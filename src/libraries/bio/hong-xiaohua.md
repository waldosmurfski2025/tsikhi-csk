**Hong Xiaohua** (Mandarin Chinese: 红小花 (Hóng Xiǎohuā); Cossackian: Рожа Ярославича Краснаева (Rozha Yaroslavicha Krasnayeva); born 8 September 2008) was the fifth Tsar of Cossackia. He led the social reforms that established the social welfare, political maneuvering, and the medical advancements of the country.
