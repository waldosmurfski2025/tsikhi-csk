**Hong Xiaohua** (Mandarin Chinese: 红小花 (Hóng Xiǎohuā); Cossackian: Рожа Ярославича Краснаева (Rozha Yaroslavicha Krasnayeva); 9 November 1696 – 19 December 1764) was the fifth Tsar of Cossackia. He led the social reforms that established the social welfare, political maneuvering, and the medical advancements of the country.
